Philosophy

These are my personal beliefs and reflect no companies I work for.

I believe that we all are valuable, that everything we do is meaningful, and that we are all part of the immense cosmic casual web. I really believe reading is good for you, any kind of more sustained and intensive reading exercises the brain and helps the mind. I think the power of any Turing-Complete programming language is truly awe-inspiring. The idea that we can use code to basically do anything we can dream up is a true enabler of the human potential. Though that power obviously could be a double-edged sword. We must very eagerly temper that power with carefully considered ethical behavior. The issue is, like Alfred Nobel and his dynamite, we can never truly know or understand what people far in the future might be able to build, upon our work. It might be that no matter the brilliant idea, some other ingenious human might come along and think of a way to make a weapon of it, or build off of it in such a way that harm comes to society and humanity.
